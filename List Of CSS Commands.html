Following is a list of the most used CSS (the language used to style websites in HTML) commands:

  • (background-color:) -Changes the background color.

  • (font-family:) -Changes the font to your link.

  • (font-size:) -Changes how big you want your font to look

  • (font-weight:) -Changes the weight of text / the boldness of the font.

  • (background-image:) -Can make an image the background of you website.

  • (color:) -Changes the color of the text.

  • (text-align:) -Aligns your text center/left/right/or justified.

  • (margin-left:) -Sets the left margin of the element you are styling

  • (margin-right:) -Sets the right margin of the element you are styling

  • (margin-bottom:) -Sets the bottom margin of the element you are styling

  • (margin-top:) -Sets the top margin of the element you are styling

  • (border-style:) -Sets the type of border you want t style (solid/dashed/etc.)

  • (border-width:) -Sets the thickness/width of a border

  • (border-color:) -Changes the color of the border you are styling

  • (Text-Decoration:) -Decorates your text (underline/linethrough/etc.)

Note: The colon (:) should always be left in its place. Then after you are done with that one specific command always end it with a semi-colon (;).
