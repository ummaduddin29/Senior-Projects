The <style> tag can let you style your website in many different ways including the font, background color, and text color. This tag will help you make your website more attractive and clean.

To style your website you should place an open <style> after the <head> tag and before the <body> tag. Right after that, type in "body" without angle brackets and two braces ({}), this means you want to style thhe entire visible website at once. Opening a <style> starts the CSS (the language used to style websites in HTML) section of your website, the CSS is the language thats styles your website.
